Michael Souffront serves as a Project Engineer at Aquaveo and manages the global GEOGLOWS ECMWF Streamflow Service. He holds a Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from Brigham Young University and an M.S. in Watershed Sciences from Utah State University. He is the recipient of the 2022 GEO Individual Excellence Award for demonstrating remarkable personal commitment to the GEO mission and vision in improving global water sustainability.
