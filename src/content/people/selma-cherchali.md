Selma Cherchali heads the Earth Observation Program at CNES and leads the Earth Observation department in the CNES Strategy Directorate. She is the Principal French Delegate at the ESA Programme Board and has chaired CEOS. In her role she manages and supports science and user communities for space missions, with an emphasis on downstream preparatory programs.
