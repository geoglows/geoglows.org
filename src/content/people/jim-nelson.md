Dr. Jim Nelson has made exceptional contributions to the advancement of water resources data around the world, pioneering scientific and global collaboration through the GEO Global Water Sustainability Initiative (GEOGLOWS) and the open sharing of Earth observation data. After a career developing capacity in water resources management in Latin America, he was introduced to GEO at the 2014 Plenary in Geneva.
