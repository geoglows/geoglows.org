Riley Hales is a researcher in hydrology and river hydraulics with an emphasis on numerical modelling. His work focuses on building large-scale, user-accessible, application-ready models for sustainable use in developing regions, spanning physical and statistical models, machine learning, remote sensing, high-performance computing, GIS, and decision-support tools.
