The Groundwater Data Mapper lets users select a geographic area and display groundwater data for selected aquifers.

## What it shows

- Well locations and metadata, including well depth, aquifer, and elevation
- Time series of measured water depths for each well
- Maps of interpolated groundwater levels, statewide or by aquifer
