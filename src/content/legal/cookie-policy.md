This Cookie Policy explains how GEOGLOWS uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. It should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy), which describes how we collect and use information about you.

## What are cookies?

- **Cookies** are small data files stored on your browser or device, set either by us (first-party) or by others (third-party). They help websites work and remember your preferences.
- **Pixels** are small images embedded in a web page or email that can collect information about your browser or device.
- **Local storage** refers to data stored locally on your device, including HTML5 local storage and the browser cache.

## How we use cookies

We use these technologies for a limited set of purposes:

- **Strictly necessary.** Required for the website to function, such as page navigation and security.
- **Functional.** Remember choices you make, such as language or display preferences.
- **Analytics and performance.** Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it, for example which pages are most useful. Where used, analytics data is aggregated.
- **Security.** Help us detect and prevent security risks and abuse.

We do not use cookies to serve third-party advertising.

## Your choices

You can control cookies in several ways:

- **Browser settings.** Most browsers let you view, delete, and block cookies. Removing or blocking cookies may affect how parts of the site work.
- **Analytics opt-out.** Where analytics tools are used, you can generally opt out through the provider's controls or your browser settings.
- **Device settings.** Mobile devices offer additional controls over local storage and tracking.
